Frequently Asked Questions

Is ClearSkin AI free to use?

New users get one free trial scan. After that, a premium subscription ($3.33 USD/month) is required for unlimited scans and full access to all features.

Is ClearSkin AI a medical device?

No. ClearSkin AI provides informational skin analysis only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical concerns.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the App's settings. You'll retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

How is my data protected?

We use encryption, row-level security, compound rate limiting, and OWASP Top 10:2025 compliant security measures. Your photos are processed by AI for analysis only and are not used to train models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You can delete your account from the App's settings at any time. This permanently removes all your personal data, scan photos, analysis results, and contact history.

What platforms is the App available on?

ClearSkin AI is available on both iOS and Android devices.
