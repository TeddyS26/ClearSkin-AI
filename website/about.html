About ClearSkin AI

Revolutionizing skincare with artificial intelligence

Our Mission

ClearSkin AI was created with a simple mission: to make advanced skin analysis accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone deserves access to personalized skincare insights, and we're using the power of artificial intelligence to make that vision a reality.

Our mobile application uses cutting-edge AI technology to analyze photos of your skin and provide detailed insights about your skin health. By leveraging advanced image recognition capabilities, we can identify various skin concerns and provide personalized recommendations.

Technology Stack

ClearSkin AI is built using modern technologies including React Native for the mobile app, Supabase for secure data storage, Stripe for payment processing, and OpenAI's API for AI-powered skin analysis. We prioritize security, privacy, and user experience in everything we build.
